About Me

Who Am I?

Hey there! I'm Dom I'm a student at Columbia College Chicago and I'm majoring in Music Technology with a concentration on Hardware and Physical Design. My passions involve all audio electronics, audio programming, and just building fun devices to make some noise with. Hope you like some of the projects on here and feel free to reach out, thanks!.

I love the whole design process of creating electronic instruments and audio devices. There is something so gratifying about slogging my way through research and work, just to see the finished project in exactly the way I hoped (if I'm lucky).
